Why PU-Connect
Built for Students,
by Students

Everything you need to trade within your university community — secure, simple, and student-first.

Student-Verified Users

Every seller and buyer is verified with a university email address — no strangers, only fellow students you can trust.

Campus-Only Listings

All listings are tied to your campus location. Arrange safe, convenient meetups at well-known spots on campus.

In-App Messaging

Chat directly with buyers or sellers without ever sharing personal contact details. Stay private, stay safe.

Secure Payments

Pay securely through Mobile Money or our escrow system. Funds are only released when both parties confirm the deal.

Ratings & Reviews

Build a reputation on campus. Rate transactions and view seller scores to make informed buying decisions.

Smart Alerts

Set up watchlists for specific items. Get notified instantly when what you need pops up on the marketplace.
